Route: `/account`
Sidebar group: (your avatar, bottom-left sidebar)
Permission: any logged-in user

## What it does

Your personal settings. Change your password, link/unlink identities.

## What you see

- **Profile**, username, role, last login.
- **Password**, change form (current + new + confirm).
- **Linked identities**, Discord, Steam, FiveM (Cfx.re). Link to allow OAuth login with that account.

## Common actions

- **Change password**, old password required, new must be 8+ chars.
- **Link Discord**, opens Discord OAuth flow.
- **Unlink**, removes that identity from your account.

## Notes

If you forget your password and can't reach the OAuth flow, you'll need shell access to reset it via the database. See [Troubleshooting](../troubleshooting#i-forgot-my-admin-password).

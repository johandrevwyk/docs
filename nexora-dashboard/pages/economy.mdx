Route: `/money`
Sidebar group: World
Permission: `money.view` (ack alerts needs `money.alerts.ack`)

## What it does

Track the total money flowing through your server and catch suspicious activity with automatic alerts.

## What you see

- **Totals panel**, combined cash, bank, crypto, dirty across all characters.
- **Transaction log**, every money change with player, amount, source, timestamp.
- **Alerts panel**, auto-generated warnings:
  - Severity badges (info / warn / critical).
  - Examples: sudden large transfer, top-1% bank balance, recent dirty money spike.

## Common actions

- **Filter** transactions by player, amount range, source.
- **Acknowledge alert**, marks it handled. Requires `money.alerts.ack`.
- **Ack all**, clears every active alert at once.
- **Click player** in a transaction → [Player Profile](/nexora-dashboard/pages/player-profile).

## Notes

Alert thresholds are tunable on [Settings](/nexora-dashboard/pages/settings). Acknowledging an alert doesn't fix the underlying transaction, it just hides it from the alert feed.

Route: `/entities`
Sidebar group: System
Permission: `entities.view`

## What it does

Tracks how many active vehicles, peds and objects are on your server right now. Useful for spotting entity bloat (too many spawned cars or props) before performance tanks.

## What you see

- **Live counts**, vehicles, peds, objects, total.
- **Sparklines**, last hour of each count.
- **Snapshots panel**, point-in-time history with timestamps.

## Common actions

- **Click sparkline** → expanded chart with longer range.
- **Force snapshot**, record current counts immediately.

## Notes

Entity caps in FiveM are around 4,000 vehicles and 12,000 total entities (varies by server). The page colors counts yellow / red as you approach those caps.
